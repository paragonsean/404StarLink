# RenewalType

Specifies whether the term of your reserved queue pricing plan is automatically extended (AUTO_RENEW) or expires (EXPIRE) at the end of the term.

## Enum

* `AUTO_RENEW` (value: `'AUTO_RENEW'`)

* `EXPIRE` (value: `'EXPIRE'`)
